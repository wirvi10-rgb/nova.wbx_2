Questions, answered 💬

Does the UV light actually work?+

Yes! The built-in UV-C light is designed to reduce everyday germs on brush heads with each cycle. It's a hygiene helper, not a medical device, and doesn't replace replacing your brush every few months.

Is it safe for kids?+

Yes — the UV chamber is fully enclosed and only activates when the drawer is closed, so there's no direct exposure during normal use.

How do I install it?+

Peel the adhesive backing, press the unit firmly to a clean, dry wall for 30 seconds, and let it set for 24 hours before loading brushes.

How often do I replace parts?+

The toothpaste cartridge typically lasts 4–6 weeks per household; replacements are available separately.
